Scripture Union Choruses

"Speak to one another in Psalms and Hymns and Spiritual Songs, singing and making melody in your heart to the Lord" - Eph.5:19.

Spiritual Songs have the ability to teach us, and admonish us, and to provoke our heart to worship our Saviour with thankfulness.

"This app is based on the Scripture Union Chorus book published by Scripture Union (Nigeria) Press and Books Ltd 2015 edition.

This app features:

- Find song by number or by title,
The title is the first line of the song

- General Index with chorus titles arranged numerically,
It is of the three C.S.S.M. Chorus Books:
No.1 - 1 to 324
No.2 - 325 to 535
No.3 - 536 to 709

- Alphabetical Index with chorus titles sorted aphabetically.

- Classified Index which categorised choruses.

Frequently used songs can be added to favourite for quick access.

The 'My Scripts' and 'Play Song Tune' features will be activated in the later premium versions.

May God's blessings shower on us as we sing songs of praise and worship to Him in Jesus' name.
